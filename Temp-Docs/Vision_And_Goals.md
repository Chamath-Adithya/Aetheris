# Veritas: The Reality Engine - Vision and Goals

## 🌍 Project Origin
Veritas is a radical, highly-specialized pivot of the Crucix/Aetheris engine. While earlier architectures focused on gathering open-source intelligence (OSINT) to predict system failures (the Antigravity Protocol), Veritas pivots this entire computational engine toward a single, critical mission: verifiable truth.

## 💡 The Innovative Idea: The Reality Engine
In the era of AI-generated deepfakes, hallucinated news, and rapid-fire geopolitical propaganda, the concept of objective reality is fracturing. People no longer know what is true, and fact-checkers rely on human consensus that is often biased or too slow.

**Veritas solves this by treating its 27 OSINT APIs not as "news feeds," but as unalterable physical planetary sensors.**

When a viral claim hits social media (e.g., *"Massive explosions and internet blackouts in City X"*), Veritas acts as an Autonomous Truth Oracle:
1. **The Claim Engine intercepts the rumor.**
2. **The Sensor Sweep begins:**
   - It checks **NASA FIRMS** for actual thermal anomalies/fires.
   - It checks **OpenSky** to see if commercial flights are deviating away from that airspace.
   - It checks **Cloudflare Radar** to verify if internet traffic has actually dropped.
   - It checks **KiwiSDR** for military radio jamming interference in the region.
3. **The Proof:** Veritas mathematically cross-references these physical data points against the claim and generates a **Reality Score (0-100%)** alongside an irrefutable "Truth Proof".

Veritas doesn't care about what people *say* is happening. It cares about what the planet's physical data *proves* is happening.

## 🎯 Vision and Goals

**Our Vision:** 
To build the world's first decentralized, autonomous fact-checking network that relies entirely on physical planetary data rather than human consensus to debunk propaganda and verify reality.

**Key Goals:**
1. **The Reality Score:** To provide mathematical confidence intervals for breaking news.
2. **Decentralized Publishing (Webhooks):** Automatically export "Truth Proofs" to ledgers, blockchains, or social media bots the second a deepfake starts going viral.
3. **Speed over Consensus:** Fact-check physical events in the 15 minutes it takes for our OSINT sweep to complete, bypassing hours or days of human deliberation.
